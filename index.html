How it works

Goal: Match symbols on 3 reels.

Bet: Choose 1–200 stones, then press Spin.

Payouts: 3 in a row pays the higher multiplier; any pair pays the pair multiplier.

Win = multiplier × bet. Winnings are added to your balance.

Reset: Returns balance to 100 stones.
